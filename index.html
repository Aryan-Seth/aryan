Chaitanya Kapoor

I am a junior at BITS Pilani, Rajasthan, majoring in Electrical and Electronics Engineering.

I am an avid machine learning enthusiast, specifically in the domains of Deep Learning and Computer Vision . Beyond this, I have a very keen interest in physics (leaning towards Quantum Mechanics). In an attempt to disseminate my love for physics and desire to help students (or instructors), I am aiming to publish a solutions manual to the seminal text on physics problems, "Problems in General Physics, I.E. Irodov" . Although still a work in progress, I try to be as regular as possible in updating and publishing my work on this website .

I attribute all my research experience and opportunities to Donglai Wei . None of this would have been possible had he not been willing to take a chance on me :-)

In my spare time, I enjoy long distance running and watching as well as playing soccer.
